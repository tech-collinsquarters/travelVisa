Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers!

How long does it take to get an Australian visa?

Most applications are processed within 48 hours. However, complex cases may take up to 5-7 business days. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

What documents do I need?

Basic requirements include a valid passport (minimum 6 months validity), recent photograph, and proof of travel purpose. We'll provide a complete checklist after your initial assessment.

Is your service legitimate?

Yes! We are registered visa consultants authorized to assist with Australian visa applications. We work directly with the Department of Home Affairs and follow all official guidelines.

What if my visa application is unsuccessful?

With our 98% success rate, unfavorable outcomes are rare. If an issue arises due to an error on our part, we work diligently to resolve it. Service terms and conditions apply.*

How much does your service cost?
